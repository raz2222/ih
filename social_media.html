Social Media Marketing

Social media marketing is a type of digital marketing where brands interact with their customers on social media in a bid to develop their reputation, boost sales, and increase website traffic.
It revolves around creating and sharing content to drive engagement.
This content can be either in text, picture, or video format. Social media marketing can also be paid and Facebook ads is probably the most popular paid social media advertising around.
Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube, and Instagram are some of the most popular social media platforms around.
As a result, they are some of the best places for brands to build a following and market themselves.

However, whether a social media platform is popular is not the most consideration when choosing a platform on which to do social media marketing.
What matters the most is where your audience is. For instance, if the majority of your audience are professionals, then LinkedIn should be the media platform where you focus most of your efforts.
If you run a fashion brand, however, or something picturesque, it is highly likely that a large number of your prospective customers will be on Instagram or Pinterest.

Social media holds several opportunities for businesses.
And in order to do it effectively, do the following:

Strategize – your social media profile/handle represents your brand.
It embodies what you are about and can influence your reputation by those who interact and come across it.
It is important for a brand to have a defined identity which is clearly communicated to its customers.
As such, develop a strategy for which kind of content will best describe your brand and also attract the kind of customers you cater to. While strategizing, also determine the best platforms to use for your marketing and define the goals you want to achieve with it.

Plan your content – after strategizing, the next thing to address is your content.
A successful social media marketing is one that is consistent in content publishing.
So, create a content plan. In it, determine which content you will post, how frequently you will publish them, and on which days.
Then set out to create them.

Engage – audience engagement is central in order to have a successful social media marketing.
Is someone saying something about your brand?
A response will go a long way toward endearing you to them.
Does someone have a suggestion about a product or service?
Listen to them, consider if they have a point, collect other opinions, and then act on it.
Listening and engaging with your audience will make all the difference between whether you remain relatively alone with little following or big and influential.

Use analytics – have you progressed or regressed in the past month? Did your reach increase?
Were the conversations around your brand positive or negative?
Did your marketing result in more sales?
These and more are the questions analytics can answer for you.
And if you have answers to them, you will be better able to adapt your strategy to achieve results.
