Content Marketing

Content marketing is a form of marketing that involves the creation and distribution of relevant and valuable content on a regular basis.
Content marketing is done to attract and gain a clearly defined audience with the aim of gaining more customers and profits.
Content marketing is highly focused on providing top value to customers with the hope that they will reward a brand with their trust and money.
Some of the examples of content marketing are infographics, webpages, podcasts, videos, and books. To better understand content marketing is, you should know about, and understand, the four steps of the buying cycle.

They are:

Awareness – sometimes, a customer has a need but is not aware of the solution to that need.
The awareness stage is where they discover that there is indeed a solution to their need(s).

Research – now that they know there is a solution, they have to perform research to know more about it.
This will better inform their knowledge as to the best course to take to resolve their need.

Consideration – the customer begins to compare different products from different companies to ensure that they are getting the best quality product at the best price.

Buy – this is the final stage of the buying cycle and it ends when the customers make their decision and complete the transaction.

Content marketing is essentially about the first two steps of this cycle; that is, awareness and research.
It makes customers aware of the solutions to some of their problems.
It also educates them about a product they have little to no knowledge of.

For a much better understanding of content marketing,
the following are some of the benefits it provides businesses:

Improved Brand Reputation Research has shown that 78% of customers prefer to know about businesses through articles rather than ads.
When the content of the articles they read is of high quality, then they develop a positive opinion of the brand, which serves as the foundation on which trust is built between both the customer and the brand, ultimately improving their brand reputation.

Generate More Leads More Affordably Lead generation is important for businesses that depend on consistent traffic in developing their brand and improve their sales.
If you post valuable contents, then you will be able to attract more leads than traditional marketing will provide you.
According to DemandMetric, content marketing is about 62% cheaper than traditional marketing, yet, it provides three times more leads.
